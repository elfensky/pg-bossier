## Output structure

Write your Round 2 critique to `docs/superpowers/debates/2026-05-23-goal-9-spec-adversarial-review/round-2-<your-name>.md`. Use this structure:

### Concessions
Specific points you concede. Name the critic and quote the relevant claim.

### Rebuttals
Specific points you push back on. Name the critic, restate their claim, and explain why it's wrong or overstated.

### Escalations
New attacks unlocked by seeing others' critiques — things you didn't see in Round 1 that are now clear.

### Final position
- One of: **SHIP AS-IS** / **SHIP WITH NAMED CHANGES** / **BLOCK UNTIL X**.
- A re-prioritized blocker list (must-land-before-merge vs nice-to-have).

Be technically rigorous. Cite Round 1 critiques by participant name. Cite the spec where appropriate. No hedging — take a clear position.

Identify yourself in the file header (e.g. `# Codex critique — Round 2`).
